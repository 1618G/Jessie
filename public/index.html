How It Works

Our Design Process

From first consultation to final flourish — a seamless journey to your perfect space

01

Discovery Call

We start with a free 15-minute call to understand your property, goals, and timeline. Whether it’s an Airbnb launch or a home that won’t sell, we’ll outline the best approach.

02

Property Assessment

Our designer visits your property for a thorough assessment. We evaluate light, flow, space, and potential. For Airbnbs, we also research your local competition and target guest demographic.

03

Design & Presentation

We create a bespoke design plan with mood boards, colour palettes, furniture selections, and 3D visualisations. You’ll see exactly how your space will look before we begin.

04

Transformation

Our team handles everything — painting, furnishing, accessorising, and styling. We coordinate trusted decorators and suppliers to deliver a turnkey result in as little as 5 working days.

05

Photography & Launch

We style your space for professional photography. For Airbnbs, we optimise your listing copy and images. For sales, we prep for estate agent viewings. Your space is ready to impress.

About Us

Meet Jessie

From Cambridge Colour Consultants to property transformations — a natural evolution in making spaces extraordinary.

Jessie’s passion for interior design has led her to become a highly experienced and sought-after colour consultant for both residential homes and businesses. With over a decade of experience, she has built an impressive portfolio across Cambridge and Essex.

Recognising the growing demand for Airbnb styling and property staging, Jessie launched Cambridge Property Styling — bringing her signature colour expertise to the world of short-term rentals and the property sales market.

Her deeply personal approach means every project starts with understanding you, your property, and your goals. Whether it’s maximising Airbnb bookings or helping a stale listing finally sell, Jessie’s eye for colour, texture, and space delivers results that speak for themselves.
